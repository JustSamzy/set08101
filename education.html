Samantha Caven - 40429097 - Mature Learner

Before I came to Edinburgh Napier, I had orignally taken the route of studying vehicle maintenance and repair at West Lothian College before progressing to Edinburgh College to study at City & Guilds level. However, the toxicity of the environment finally wore me down and I decided to persue other interests. I was approached by a lecturer in the Creative Arts department who encouraged me to look into Audio Visual Technology, so I did. Even though this was another male dominated field, AV Tech was encouraging females to join the field and it was a much more accepting environment. I progressed all the way to a HND in Audio Visual Technology before deciding it was time to take a break from education and get a job. This is where I fell back in love with computing and all things technology, as I worked in Maplin Electronics. However, I got bored just working and ended up taking on extra responsibilities such as learning through First4Skills, becoming Twitter and Smart Home champion, working in other stores across Scotland and getting promoted to Sales Team Leader. Without this amazing job, I dont think I would have taken the plunge to go back to College to study Computing and then university. Unforanately, as you are probably aware, Maplin fell into administration and I lost my job and it become an online only retailer eventually. So far, that job has been the best job I have ever had and it will be hard to beat! I have never felt ready or "smart enough" for university but studying Computing, something I am passionate about, going to university became less scary and more exciting. Coming to university when I did meant I could get a degree in Cyber and Forensics which will in turn hopefully get me into the forensics field. So far I have completed Networked Services, Scripting for Cyber and Networks and Network Security and Cryptography. I am looking forward to completing my other classes with the intention of progress into the Honours degree.
